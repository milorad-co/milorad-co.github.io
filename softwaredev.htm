Milorad Software Development

Milorad is currently developing six softwares; GUMP, KUTE, Dum Dum Fait, SuppaTaxKard, Gisnep-, and MIV.

GUMP (GNU Useless Manipulation Program)

GUMP is Milorad's art program and Milorad's main focus currently.

KUTE (KDE Useless Text Editor)

KUTE is Milorad's text editor and a side-quest for development.

Dum Dum Fait

A card game adapted into video game form. Won't be made in a long time.

SuppaTaxKard

A mod for SuperTuxKart. Almost complete.

Gisnep-

Information will be saved for a future date.

MIV

A high speed viewer for MIF files due to GUMP's terrible loading speeds.
