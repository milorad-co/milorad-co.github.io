Section g. domestic labour and economics

0) Delta (Δ) is the official currency of peteland, with a fixed conversion rate of Δ1.5 to NZ$1.
1) The minimum wage of Peteland is Δ30 per hour.
2) For all jobs that are not vital (education, government, emergency services), you cannot be forced to work more than four days every week.
3) If your job is digital, you never have to arrive at work.
4) The first day you arrive at work each week, you must be given a cup of coffee if you want one.
5) Churches must pay taxes like any other service.
6) All forms of income (including donations), NOT profit, is taxed 12%. Nothing else is taxed in any way by the government.
