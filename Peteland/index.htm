Peteland Laws and Administration

Peteland laws are divided into nine sections, ordered by priority, from a to i. Laws are referenced by the law number, then the section the law is in. For example, law 0a is law 0 of section a.
a. core
b. military and general firearms usage
c. crime and punishment
d. education
e. healthcare
f. foreign relations and transport
g. domestic labour and economics
h. food, drink, and other substances
i. identity
