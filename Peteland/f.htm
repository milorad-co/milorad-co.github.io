Section f. foreign relations and transport

0) Those who wish to enter the country may not bring organic food, alcohol, drugs, tabacco, vapes, caffinated drinks other than coffee, seeds of invasive plants, firearms, or toxic substances.


1) Those inside the country may be arrested and detained in a Peteland jail cell if they break the law. They may also be deported instead.
2) If you wish to gain citizenship of Peteland, you must be approved by the Leader.
