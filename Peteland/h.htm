Section h. food, drink, and other substances

0) The sale or donation of Weetbix, alcohol, tabacco, drugs, and caffinated drinks other than coffee, is banned. Giving them to others in private is okay, as well as producing it yourself. Faliure to comply is a Medium Severity crime and results in a punishment of a one year sentence, confiscation of all of the items listed, and a Δ3000 fine.
1) If you are caught consuming one of the substances listed in 0h, you must tell the Police who gave you those substances and where they gave you them or else face a charge of a Minimal Severity crime with a punishment of a Δ150 fine.
